## About Me

I'm a final-year Computer Science student at **Swinburne University of Technology**, majoring in **Cyber Security**, based in Malaysia. I approach technology holistically — I don't just study security, I live it: building home networks, assembling and repairing systems, and translating that hands-on curiosity into a growing portfolio of industry certifications from **AWS**, **Cisco**, and **EC-Council**.

My engineering interests sit at the intersection of **cybersecurity, AI/ML, and full-stack development** — from threat management and digital forensics to OSINT and cloud security, with a growing focus on applying AI to strengthen defensive security operations. My capstone project, **Datalytic.ai**, was recognized with the **Best CAPSTONE Innovation Award**, and I placed **1st Runner-Up at the AWS-INTI Ideathon 2025**.

Beyond the terminal, I lead with purpose — serving as **Vice President of Leo Club of IICS Metro** and an **INTI Student Ambassador**, where I've learned that great engineering, like great leadership, is built on trust, documentation, and consistency.
